Basically, What is Graphic Design?

Graphic design is a communication design; it’s an art & practice of planning and projecting ideas and experiences with textual and visual content. The experience takes place in an instant and over a long period. Graphic designers create and combine symbols, images and text to form visual representations of ideas and messages. Design that’s meant to be experienced instantly is the easiest to recognise and has been around the longest. For the last century, designers have arranged type, form advertisements, and images on posters, packages and other printed equipment, and information of designs visualisations and graphics for newspapers and magazines.
